# Testing Scenarios

## Scenario 1: Optimal Conditions

**Inputs**

* Temperature: 25°C
* Humidity: 85%
* CO₂: 700 ppm

**Expected Result**

* Moderate or high yield prediction
* No validation warnings

---

## Scenario 2: Low Humidity

**Inputs**

* Temperature: 25°C
* Humidity: 40%
* CO₂: 700 ppm

**Expected Result**

* Yield lower than optimal conditions
* Humidity warning displayed

---

## Scenario 3: High Temperature

**Inputs**

* Temperature: 35°C
* Humidity: 85%
* CO₂: 700 ppm

**Expected Result**

* Yield may decrease
* Temperature warning displayed

---

## Scenario 4: Extreme CO₂

**Inputs**

* Temperature: 25°C
* Humidity: 85%
* CO₂: 1500 ppm

**Expected Result**

* Prediction generated successfully
* CO₂ warning displayed

---

## Scenario 5: Multiple Out-of-Range Inputs

**Inputs**

* Temperature: 35°C
* Humidity: 40%
* CO₂: 1500 ppm

**Expected Result**

* Prediction generated successfully
* Multiple warnings displayed
* Application remains stable
